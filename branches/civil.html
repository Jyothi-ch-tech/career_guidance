All about the Civil branch at CBIT

📌 What is Civil?

Civil Engineering deals with construction, design, and maintenance of infrastructure like buildings, roads, bridges, and dams. It’s a highly practical and field-oriented branch.

  • Structural Analysis, Concrete Technology
  • Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering
  • Hydraulics, Environmental Engineering
  • Surveying, Construction Management
