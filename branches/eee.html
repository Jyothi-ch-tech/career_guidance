Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)

All about the EEE branch at CBIT

📌 What is EEE?

EEE is the study of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It focuses on power systems, circuits, control systems, and electrical machines.

📚 Subjects by Semester

  • Electrical Circuits, Machines, Power Systems
  • Control Systems, Power Electronics
  • Microcontrollers, Renewable Energy
  • Embedded Systems, MATLAB

🏢 Companies That Visit CBIT

  • NTPC, Siemens, ABB, L&T
  • TCS, Infosys, Tech Mahindra (IT roles)
  • ISRO, BHEL, ECIL (via GATE/PSU)

🛠 Focus Areas / Skills

  • Power Systems, Control Engineering
  • Electrical Machines, Circuit Design
  • MATLAB, Simulink, PLC/SCADA

🎯 Job Roles

  • Power Engineer
  • Electrical Design Engineer
  • System Control Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Developer
