📌 What is Biotechnology?

Biotechnology is the application of biology and technology to create solutions in medicine, agriculture, environment, and research. It’s a perfect blend of engineering and life sciences.

  • Bioprocess Engineering, Immunology

  • Enzyme Technology, Biochemistry
