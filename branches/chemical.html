📌 What is Chemical Engineering?

Chemical Engineering involves the design, development, and operation of processes for large-scale chemical manufacturing. It blends chemistry, physics, and mathematics with engineering principles to create efficient, safe, and sustainable production systems.

📚 Subjects by Semester

  • Fluid Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Chemical Process Calculations
  • Heat Transfer, Mass Transfer, Reaction Engineering
  • Plant Design, Process Control, Environmental Engineering
  • Industrial Safety, Simulation, Instrumentation

🎯 Career Options

  • Process Engineer
  • Plant Design Engineer
  • Safety & Environmental Engineer
  • Production Manager
  • R&D Scientist

❓ Common Misconceptions

  • “Chemical Engineering means chemistry every day” ❌ — It’s more about process and system design
  • “It has no scope” ❌ — It has a huge demand in oil, pharma, and sustainability sectors
