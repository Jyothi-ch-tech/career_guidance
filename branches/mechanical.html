Mechanical Engineering (MECH)

All about the Mechanical branch at CBIT

📌 What is Mechanical?

Mechanical Engineering deals with machines, engines, robotics, and manufacturing processes. It’s one of the oldest and broadest branches in engineering.

📚 Subjects by Semester

  • Engineering Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Strength of Materials
  • Fluid Mechanics, Heat Transfer, Machine Design
  • Robotics, CAD/CAM, Mechatronics
  • Manufacturing Technology, IC Engines, FEM

🏢 Companies That Visit CBIT

  • Mahindra, L&T, Tata Motors, Ashok Leyland
  • Infosys, TCS (for software roles)
  • ISRO, BHEL, DRDO (via GATE/PSU)

🛠 Focus Areas / Skills

  • Design and Manufacturing
  • CAD Tools (AutoCAD, SolidWorks)
  • Thermal Engineering
  • Mechanical Analysis & Simulation

🎯 Job Roles

  • Design Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Production Manager
  • R&D Engineer
