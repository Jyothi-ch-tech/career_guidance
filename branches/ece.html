Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

All about the ECE branch at CBIT

📌 What is ECE?

ECE focuses on electronics, communication systems, VLSI, embedded systems, and signal processing. It's a versatile branch that bridges both hardware and software, allowing students to explore both core and IT job sectors.

📚 Subjects by Semester

  • Digital Electronics, Analog Circuits, Signals & Systems
  • Microprocessors, Communication Systems, VLSI Design
  • Embedded Systems, IoT, Antennas, Satellite Communication
  • MATLAB, HDL (Verilog/VHDL), Robotics

🏢 Companies That Visit CBIT for ECE

  • Intel, Qualcomm, Texas Instruments
  • Infosys, TCS, Accenture, Capgemini
  • DRDO, ISRO (through exams)

🛠 Focus Areas / Skills

  • VLSI and Chip Design
  • Communication Systems (Wireless, Optical)
  • Embedded Programming (C, ARM)
  • Signal Processing, MATLAB

🎯 Job Roles

  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Design Engineer (VLSI)
  • Signal Processing Engineer
  • Software Developer (via IT roles)

❓ Common Misconceptions

  • “ECE students can’t get IT jobs” ❌ — Many get placed in top software companies
  • “It’s all hardware” ❌ — Software skills are equally important (MATLAB, Verilog, etc.)
